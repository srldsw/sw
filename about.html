About Sitesworld

Sitesworld.com is an international one-stop quick-reference guide to various live-resources around the internet, for almost every country in the world.

This website was created in order to gather, collect and compile in one place, various scattered international resources available all over the internet for syndication, and for ease of reference and a one-page convenient internet resource.

Sitesworld.com country guides should be seen as an instant information booklet, pamphlet or even a brochure for any given country, for any given day. You will find top websites, latest news, fresh photos, social bookmarks, printed journals, blog posts and more! We have even included a special Google search box specific to each country's particular worldwide web. Each page contains a small country introduction for some quick facts.

We are constantly looking for new and exciting resources and will be updating our pages with more happening and current resources. We are also adding, updating or modifying our website and relevant country specific pages, so do come back often. Some of our plans include a weblog and a forum among others, and we hope to have a more interactive content soon.
