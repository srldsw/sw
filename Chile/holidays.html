Chilean Holidays

Various public & national holidays observed in Chile

﻿
sitesworld.comMonthDayEnglish NameLocal NameRemarks
January1 (Tuesday)New Year's Day Año Nuevo Blue laws forbid opening of most services.
February No holidays
March29 (Friday)Good Friday Viernes Santo
30 (Saturday)Holy Saturday Sábado Santo
April No holidays
May1 (Wednesday)Labour Day Día del Trabajo On this day, more stores and services will close than on any other holiday, as stipulated by blue laws.
21 (Tuesday)Navy Day Día de las Glorias Navales Commemorates the Battle of Iquique. Annual speech by the President to Congress.
June7 (Friday)Battle of Arica Asalto y Toma del Morro de Arica This is a regional holiday, valid only in the Arica and Parinacota Region .
29 (Saturday)Saint Peter and Saint Paul San Pedro y San Pablo
30 (Sunday)Primary elections Elecciones primarias Employers are required to give employees two hours off work.
July16 (Tuesday)Our Lady of Mount Carmel Virgen del Carmen Established in 2007, it replaced Corpus Christi. The Catholic Church's official name for this day is Solemnidad de la Virgen del Carmen, Reina y Patrona de Chile , but the law that created this holiday (20,148) doesn't use it.
August15 (Thursday)Assumption of Mary Asunción de la Virgen
September18 (Wednesday)National holiday Fiestas Patrias Celebrates the establishment of the First Government Junta in 1810.
19 (Thursday)Army Day Día de las Glorias del Ejército
20 (Friday)"Bridge" holiday Fiestas Patrias Law 20,215 declares a holiday on days that are Monday September 17 or Friday September 20.
October12 (Saturday)Columbus Day Día del Descubrimiento de Dos Mundos Colloquially known as Día de la Raza ("Race Day") or formally by its older name ( Aniversario del Descubrimiento de América ).
31 (Thursday)Reformation Day Día Nacional de las Iglesias Evangélicas y Protestantes Established in 2008. This holiday moves in relation to the day of week.
November1 (Friday)All Saints Día de Todos los Santos
17 (Sunday)presidential, parliamentary and regional elections elecciones presidenciales, parlamentarias y regionales Employers are required to give employees two hours off work.
December8 (Sunday)Immaculate Conception Inmaculada Concepción
15 (Sunday)run-off presidential election segunda vuelta de la elección presidencial This holiday will take place only if the run-off election is held.
25 (Wednesday)Christmas Day Navidad / Natividad del Señor
31 (Tuesday)Banking holiday Feriado bancario de fin de año Banks and other financial institutions close down. Workers in other fields are not given the day off as in the rest of the holidays, though the workday schedule is usually shorter.sitesworld.com
